Let's begin with a story

of informatics student who is trying to solve a long time everyday problem: managing knowledge for various purposes in a rapid way

  • Just a side story before and after getting problem around managin everyday knowledge.
  • And then I discovered the next stage of knowledge management and software/web development workflow and more.

Problems? [1]

Most knowledge information that we know are scattered across different documents, applications, and systems

Problems? [2]

Most people don't know how and where to start reading information or gathering knowledge for specific task, project, or development

Problems? [3]

Most tools are complicated or limited, also only lives in one system

Problems? [4]

Most text notes are not accurate, less precise, or not informative enough

Problems? [5]

Most systems remain just when online only and stuck within proprietary system

Problems? [6]

Most services are too specific, leading to more apps needed to do something that is related but outside the functionality

Problems? [7]

Most user experience in current available software are bad

Problems? [8]

Most contribution flow in Wiki is too ridiculously confusing and disorganized to understand for most people, make it hard to actually or have desire to contribute

Wow, a solution!

Introducing...

Satellid

What is Satellid?

“Rapid Micro Knowledge Manager that makes people or organization manage knowledge better and fast ”

Speaking for detail, Satellid is...

  • for managing any kind of knowledge
  • a Rapid Micro Knowledge Management System and Platform
  • scientifically defined as a Modular, Structured, Linked, and Transcluded Micro Knowledge Management System/Platform Using Web Technology with Node.js and JSON-LD.

Main Principle of Satellid

“Every knowledge is modularly small, must be structured, linked to each others, and can transclude of various unit of data or information”

Principles of Satellid

  1. Micro
  2. Linked
  3. Modular
  4. Portable
  5. Structured
  6. Decentralized
  7. Template based
  8. Knowledge oriented
